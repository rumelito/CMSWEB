CDM LinkUp

ABOUT US

CDM Linkup is being developed to address the need for a unified communication and engagement platform within our school. The existing channels for announcements, events, and other important updates are scattered, leading to information gaps and reduced student participation. By implementing CDM Linkup, we aim to create a centralized content management system that allows students, teachers, and administrators to connect, communicate, and share information seamlessly. This platform will simplify announcements, event management, and school shop inventory, making it easier for everyone to stay informed and engaged.

objective

The purpose of CDM Linkup is to provide a central platform for the school community that encourages communication, collaboration, and active participation. By offering a space where students, teachers, and administrators can easily share and access important school information, CDM Linkup aims to strengthen the sense of community within the school. The platform is designed to improve student involvement in activities, events, and updates, making school life more engaging and organized.
